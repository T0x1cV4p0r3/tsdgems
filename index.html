Getting Started with TSDMEDIAGEMS

Buy Equipment Packs: Get started by purchasing Equipment Packs on NeftyBlocks. Each pack contains utility NFTs that power your mining experience.

Stake on WAXDAO: Stake your NFTs on WAXDAO to begin earning $TSDM Tokens – the official TSDMEDIA Mining Token.

Stake $TSDM on TacoSwap: Use your earned tokens to stake on TacoSwap. The more you contribute to the Liquidity Pool, the greater your share — and the higher your chances of receiving NFT drops.

Earn Unpolished Gems: By staking, you can randomly receive raw, uncut gemstone NFTs — digital treasures that are ready to be refined. 15 NFTs distributed daily.

Polish Your Gems: Use the Polishing Table NFT to transform rough gems into stunning, polished masterpieces.

⚠️ Be careful: there is a 10% chance your gem could break during polishing, leaving behind a Gem Shard NFT.

About the Collection

TSDMEDIAGEMS is a premium NFT collection that brings well-known gemstones into digital form. The collection includes a range of designs, from raw, uncut stones to precisely cut and polished versions. Each NFT is individually created and visually detailed, offering unique variations in shape and color.

The focus is on high-quality digital craftsmanship and collectible value. TSDGEMS offers a new way to collect and own gemstones — digitally, securely, and without physical limitations.

Our Vision for the Collection

At TSDMEDIAGEMS, we're building more than just a digital gem collection — we're creating a sustainable ecosystem that puts community governance at its core.

🏗️ Two-Year Strategic Framework

Our mining farms are designed with a two-year operational term, providing stability and long-term value for our community. This structured approach ensures consistent token distribution while maintaining the integrity of our ecosystem.

🪙 TSDM Token: Distribution Mechanism

The TSDM token serves as our primary NFT distribution mechanism. Rather than being a speculative asset, it's designed to facilitate the fair allocation of digital assets within our ecosystem. We hold 50% of all tokens, ensuring we maintain alignment with our community's interests.

🗳️ Community-Driven Governance

Following the collection launch, we will implement a community voting system that puts the future of our token allocation directly in your hands. This democratic approach ensures that major decisions about token utilization are made collectively by those who are most invested in our ecosystem's success.

🚀 Ecosystem Expansion Plans

We have ambitious plans to build additional features and utilities around this collection. These developments will be guided by community feedback and designed to enhance the overall value proposition of the TSDMEDIAGEMS ecosystem.

Join us in shaping the future of digital gem collecting — where community meets innovation.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about TSDMEDIAGEMS and our ecosystem.

How do I get started with TSDMEDIAGEMS?

+

Start by purchasing Equipment Packs on NeftyBlocks, then stake your NFTs on WAXDAO to earn TSDM tokens. Use these tokens to stake on TacoSwap for a chance to receive unpolished gem NFTs. Polish your gems using the Polishing Table NFT to create stunning polished masterpieces!

What are the risks of polishing gems?

+

There is a 10% chance your gem could break during polishing, leaving behind a Gem Shard NFT. This adds an element of risk and excitement to the polishing process. The rarer the gem, the higher the stakes!

How does the gem exchange system work?

+

You can burn your gem NFTs in our exchange system to receive various rewards including Equipment Packs, WAX tokens, TDTT tokens (from our Thesaurum partnership), and special rewards like raffles with real diamond prizes. The rarer the gem, the better the rewards!

What is the difference between polished and unpolished gems?

+

Unpolished gems are raw, uncut stones that you receive from staking. Polished gems are the final, beautifully refined versions created using the Polishing Table NFT. Polished gems generally have higher value and better exchange rates.

How much can I earn with mining equipment?

+

Earnings range from 0.2083 TSDM/hour (Pickaxe Worker) to 52.8542 TSDM/hour (Large Mine). Daily earnings can reach up to 1,268.50 TSDM with the highest-tier equipment. Higher-tier equipment generates significantly more tokens!

Where can I get more information and updates?

+

Join our Discord community for the latest updates, exchange rates, upcoming events, and exclusive information. Our Discord is the central hub for all TSDMEDIAGEMS activities and community discussions!

Why is the liquidity of TSDM tokens so low?

+

TSDM tokens are designed to be earned through mining operations, not purchased on the open market. The low liquidity reflects our ecosystem's design philosophy where tokens are generated through active participation in the mining process using equipment NFTs. In the current situation, the TSDM token is not to be viewed as a profit token - it's only for mining utility within our ecosystem.
